Advantages of Improved Kienyeji Breeds

Improved breeds show increased productivity, producing more eggs, being heavier, and having higher disease resistance compared to local breeds.

Disadvantages of Improved Kienyeji

Challenges in hatching eggs and maintaining a sustainable breeding flock. Higher mortality rate due to fast growth.
